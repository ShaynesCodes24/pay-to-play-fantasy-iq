Privacy Policy

fantasyiq collects only the information needed to configure and support a customer dashboard.

  • Customer setup information may include name, email, ESPN league ID, season, league name, draft date, and logo.
  • Payment information is handled by the payment provider and is not stored in this dashboard package.
  • ESPN league data is fetched from ESPN public league endpoints when live sync is enabled.
  • Support requests should be sent to shayneholladay@gmail.com.
